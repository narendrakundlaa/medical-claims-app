Claim Status Definitions
  • New - Claim is with the employee.
  • Submitted -Claim is submitted to HRSC.
  • Resubmitted - Claim re-submitted by employee to HRSC.
  • Claim received and inprocess - Claim sent to insurance company.
  • Partially approved - Claimed amount is partially approved by insurance company.
  • Refer back - Claim referred back to employee by insurance company.
  • Information required - Additional documents required by insurance company.
  • Completed - Claim fully approved.
  • Disabled - The claim is disabled as it has exceeded its processing timelines and thus it can not be reprocessed now. For any clarifications please refer to the Medical Insurance Policy.
  • Denied - Claim denied as per policy.
